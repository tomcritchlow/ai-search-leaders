The landscape of search is shifting rapidly towards agentic AI. This article explores how autonomous agents will navigate the web on our behalf, fundamentally changing the SEO landscape.

> "The user intent is no longer just informational; it's transactional and executional."

This is a critical insight. We need to prepare for a world where our content is consumed not just by humans, but by agents looking to complete tasks.

### Key Takeaways
- Agents act as intermediaries.
- Structure data is more important than ever.
- API-first content delivery is the future.
